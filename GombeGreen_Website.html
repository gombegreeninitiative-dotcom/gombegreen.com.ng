About Gombe Green

More than recycling.
A new economy.

We are building the infrastructure, community systems, and digital technology to transform how Gombe State manages waste — creating livelihoods, reducing emissions, and positioning the region as a leader in climate-smart development.

♻ Waste Recovery
Community sorting hubs process plastics, organics, and metals for resale and composting.
📱 REMASOL Platform
Smart digital platform managing logistics, EcoPoints rewards, and real-time climate tracking.
👥 Youth Employment
100+ wastepreneurs trained and employed in collection, sorting, and data recording.
🌍 Carbon Markets
Verified carbon offset data opens access to climate finance and Net-Zero markets.

The Programme

How It Works

Six integrated components working together to create a self-sustaining waste economy.

01
🎓
Train Wastepreneurs
Local youth are trained in waste collection, sorting, value recovery, and carbon data recording using the REMASOL platform — creating skilled, employed community members.
02
📲
Deploy REMASOL
Our smart waste platform manages route logistics, distributes digital EcoPoints rewards, and tracks recyclables and climate impact data in real time.
03
🏭
Establish Sorting Hubs
Community sorting hubs process plastics, organics, and metals for resale, composting, or eco-manufacturing — creating local circular economy value chains.
04
🎁
Incentivize Households
Community members earn EcoPoints for segregated waste, redeemable for food, airtime, or education support — driving sustained household participation.
05
🏫
Mobilize Schools
Youth-led environmental stewardship through SDG-focused Green Clubs, inter-school competitions, and community education campaigns.
06
🏪
Federal Marketplace
A digital exchange enabling stakeholders to register waste reduction activities — unlocking green income, carbon offset trading, and Net-Zero participation for businesses and communities.
